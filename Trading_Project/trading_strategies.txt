
1. Simple Moving Average (SMA) Crossover: Buy when the 50-day SMA crosses above the 200-day SMA, sell when it crosses below.
2. Exponential Moving Average (EMA) Crossover: Buy when the 50-day EMA crosses above the 200-day EMA, sell when it crosses below.
3. Relative Strength Index (RSI): Buy when RSI is below 30 (oversold), sell when RSI is above 70 (overbought).
4. Bollinger Bands: Buy when the price touches the lower Bollinger Band, sell when the price touches the upper Bollinger Band.
5. Stochastic Oscillator: Buy when the %K line crosses above the %D line, sell when it crosses below.
6. Parabolic SAR: Buy when the price crosses above the Parabolic SAR, sell when it crosses below.
7. Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD): Buy when the MACD line crosses above the signal line, sell when it crosses below.
8. On-Balance Volume (OBV): Buy when OBV is increasing, sell when it is decreasing.
9. Money Flow Index (MFI): Buy when MFI is below 20, sell when it is above 80.
10. Commodity Channel Index (CCI): Buy when CCI is below -100, sell when it is above 100.
11. Accumulation/Distribution Line (A/D Line): Buy when the A/D line is rising, sell when it is falling.
12. Average Directional Index (ADX): Buy when ADX is above 25 and +DI crosses above -DI, sell when +DI crosses below -DI.
13. Fibonacci Retracement: Buy at the 38.2% retracement level, sell at the 61.8% retracement level.
14. Volume Weighted Average Price (VWAP): Buy when the price is above the VWAP, sell when it is below.
15. Chaikin Money Flow (CMF): Buy when CMF is above 0, sell when it is below 0.
16. Williams %R: Buy when %R is below -80, sell when it is above -20.
17. Keltner Channels: Buy when the price touches the lower Keltner Channel, sell when it touches the upper Keltner Channel.
18. Donchian Channels: Buy when the price breaks above the 20-day Donchian Channel, sell when it breaks below.

20. Elder Ray Index: Buy when the Bull Power is above the Bear Power, sell when Bear Power is above Bull Power.
